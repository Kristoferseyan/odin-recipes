Chicken Afritada

Descirption

This is a type of Filipino chicken stew cooked in a tomato based sauce. It usually has potato and carrot. This recipe, in particular, also makes use of red and green bell peppers.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ lb. chicken
  • 1 small red bell pepper
  • 1 small green bell pepper
  • 1 medium baking potato
  • 1 medium carrot
  • 1 8 oz. can tomato sauce
  • 1 Knorr Chicken Cube
  • 4 to 5 pieces dried bay leaves
  • 1 small onion
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 1 ½ cups water
  • 3 tablespoons cooking oil
  • Salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat the oil in a cooking pot.
  2. Sauté the garlic and onion
  3. Add the chicken pieces when the onion gets soft. Cook both sides until it turns light brown.
  4. Add the tomato sauce and water. Let boil.
  5. Put the dried bay leaves in the pot and then add the Knorr Chicken Cube. Stir and cover. Cook between low to medium heat for 30 minutes or until the chicken gets tender.
  6. Add the carrot, bell peppers, and potato. Stir. Cover and cook for 12 minutes.
  7. Add salt and ground black pepper to taste. Stir and cook for 3 minutes more.
  8. Transfer to serving bowl or plate. Serve!
